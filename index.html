About me

My Background

Although my creative career began in the late 90s in my grandmother's art studio, it took a slight detour after I studied accounting and Spanish in college, when I went to work as an auditor for EY and, later, an accounting manager for Equifax.

While these jobs mostly satisfied my curiosity itch, they did nothing for my need to be creative, so I decided to change my career path and enroll in a 6-month intensive UX/UI Design bootcamp at Georgia Tech, which has been a fantastic decision.

I currently work for CGI in their human-centered design and Strategic consulting team and work with clients to create innovative solutions with their users in mind.

When I'm not working, you can find me throwing pots in a ceramic studio or out in the wild taking pictures and/or eating food.
